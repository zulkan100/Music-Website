About Us

Who are We?

Welcome to Sterdy's Avenue, a musical haven nestled in the heart of the city. As you step through the doors of Sterdy's Avenue, you are welcomed by the harmonious blend of melodies resonating from the diverse collection of instruments lining the shelves. Sterdy's Avenue caters to musicians of all genres and skill levels. The store invites customers to explore the endless possibilities of sound, with knowledgeable and passionate staff ready to assist in finding the perfect instrument to amplify their musical journey.

At Sterdy's Avenue, music is more than a transaction; it's a shared passion that unites a community of musicians. The store not only offers an extensive range of high-quality instruments but also hosts regular events, workshops, and jam sessions, transforming Sterdy's Avenue into a vibrant hub where artists can connect, collaborate, and find inspiration.

Founders

Franz Listz, the visionary founder of Sterdy's Avenue, is a luminary in the world of music retail. A passionate and discerning musician himself, Franz's journey into the creation of Sterdy's Avenue began with a profound belief in the transformative power of music. Drawing inspiration from his namesake, the legendary composer Franz Liszt, Franz Listz embarked on a mission to curate a music store that would echo the spirit of innovation and artistry.

Franz's deep understanding of the musical landscape is reflected in every facet of Sterdy's Avenue. His commitment to quality and authenticity permeates the store's collection, ensuring that each instrument is not just a product but a conduit for artistic expression. With an unwavering dedication to fostering a vibrant musical community , Franz Listz has shaped Sterdy's Avenue into more than just a retail space.
